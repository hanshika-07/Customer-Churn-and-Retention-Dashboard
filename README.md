# Customer-Churn-and-Retention-Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn, retention, and revenue risk to help businesses identify at-risk customers and improve retention strategies.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Power Query

## KPIs
- Total Customers: 6K
- Churn Rate: 50.9%
- Retention Rate: 49.1%
- Repeat Purchase Rate: 97.5%
- Revenue at Risk: ₹3M

## Key Insights
- High-value customers represent 4.2% of customers but generate 48.7% of total revenue.
- Churn rate is 50.9%, indicating significant customer loss.
- ₹3M revenue is at risk due to customer churn.
- Retaining high-value customers can significantly improve revenue stability.

## Business Recommendations
- Implement targeted retention campaigns for high-value customers.
- Monitor customer segments with high churn risk.
- Improve customer engagement and loyalty programs.
- Analyze reasons behind churn to reduce future revenue loss.
